Wedding Venue in Bengaluru

        

  

Samavana

             Halls & Gardens

Nestled away from the casual passerby's gaze lies a meticulously curated wedding venue that seamlessly blends open and enclosed outdoor spaces, with each area exuding tranquility and intrigue. Every detail is thoughtfully placed, orchestrating an experience that evokes a true sense of place. The venue's allure lies in its ability to captivate the imagination, drawing guests into a world where tranquility meets curiosity, creating an unforgettable backdrop for a celebration filled with magic and wonder.

Our Spaces

Gulmohar Court

Nestled serenely below, our lower-level lawn at the wedding venue boasts a natural, intimate charm attached to Maya the reception hall. Towering trees offer natural shade, while the carefully curated landscaping provides a picturesque setting for guests. It provides a picturesque setting and a cozy atmosphere making it an enchanting space for personalized setups and intimate ceremonies or for guests to mingle amidst the beauty of nature, creating timeless memories in a secluded, idyllic setting.

  • Seating Capacity:
  • Sit down: 120
  • Theatre: 200
  • Cocktail: 260

Magnolia Square

At the heart of our enchanting wedding venue on the upper level, this grand lawn stands as a majestic centerpiece, boasting sprawling greenery and an expansive landscape that captivates the eye. Its vast expanse offers a canvas for unforgettable ceremonies or lavish receptions under the open sky, where guests are embraced by the natural beauty and tranquility of the surroundings. With ample space for creativity and personalization, this larger-than-life lawn promises a breathtaking backdrop for the most cherished moments of your special day.

  • Seating Capacity:
  • Sit down: 200
  • Theatre: 350
  • Cocktail: 350

Fern Garden

The adjacent lawn to the Magnolia Square is a picturesque haven, seamlessly connected to the dining space, where nature's beauty intertwines with celebration. Lush greenery and vibrant blooms create a serene backdrop to revel in the enchantment of the occasion.

  • Seating Capacity:
  • Sit down: 200

Satva

Picture a dining hall nestled beneath a graceful canopy, its roof stretching out like a protective embrace. The open sides invite the surrounding scenery to join in, offering panoramic views that shift with the passing breeze. Patrons relish their meals amidst a fusion of fresh air, serene vistas, and the comforting shelter of the overhead canopy, making each dining experience an enchanting blend of nature and culinary delight.

  • Seating Capacity:
  • Sit down: 210
  • Theatre: 240
  • Cocktail: 350

Maya

The reception hall exudes an enchanting charm that seamlessly blends nature's embrace with modern elegance. Its spacious design boasts a covered roof that extends gracefully, providing shelter while allowing the gentle caress of natural light to filter through. The sweeping views of the adjoining lawn create an idyllic backdrop. The harmonious fusion of indoor comfort and outdoor allure offers a versatile space for gatherings, whether under the sheltered canopy or amid the fresh, open-air ambiance. Whether bathed in the golden glow of daylight or adorned with twinkling lights under the starry night sky, this reception hall with its covered roof and open sides, coupled with the adjacent verdant lawn, promises an unforgettable experience where the beauty of nature meets the sophistication of celebration.

  • Seating Capacity:
  • Sit down: 160
  • Theatre: 300
  • Cocktail: 400
